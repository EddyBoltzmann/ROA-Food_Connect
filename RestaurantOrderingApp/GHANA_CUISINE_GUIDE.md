## 🍚 Traditional Ghanaian Dishes

### **Waakye** - The National Dish
- **Description**: Rice and beans cooked together with sorghum leaves, served with traditional accompaniments
- **Price Range**: ₵30-35
- **Accompaniments**: Gari, shito (pepper sauce), boiled eggs, fried plantain, meat/fish
- **Customizations**: Choice of protein (goat, beef, chicken, fish), extra gari, extra shito
- **Preparation Time**: 20-25 minutes
- **Calories**: 500-520

### **Banku** - Fermented Dough Delight
- **Description**: Fermented corn and cassava dough served with grilled fish and pepper sauce
- **Price Range**: ₵28-35
- **Accompaniments**: Grilled tilapia, pepper sauce, onions
- **Customizations**: Fish size (small, medium, large), sauce heat level
- **Preparation Time**: 25-30 minutes
- **Calories**: 450-480

### **Fufu** - Pounded Perfection
- **Description**: Pounded cassava and plantain served with various soups
- **Price Range**: ₵32-40
- **Soup Options**: Light soup, palm nut soup, groundnut soup
- **Customizations**: Soup type, meat choice (goat, beef, chicken, fish)
- **Preparation Time**: 30-35 minutes
- **Calories**: 420-450

### **Jollof Rice** - The Pride of Ghana
- **Description**: Aromatic rice cooked with tomatoes, onions, and traditional spices
- **Price Range**: ₵25-30
- **Accompaniments**: Grilled chicken, fried chicken, chicken wings
- **Customizations**: Chicken style, spice level (mild, medium, hot)
- **Preparation Time**: 20-25 minutes
- **Calories**: 400-420

### **Red Red** - Vegetarian Delight
- **Description**: Black-eyed peas in palm oil sauce served with fried ripe plantain
- **Price Range**: ₵18-25
- **Accompaniments**: Fried ripe plantain, gari, optional fried fish
- **Customizations**: Plantain style, extra palm oil, gari, fried fish
- **Preparation Time**: 15-20 minutes
- **Calories**: 350-380

### **Kelewele** - Spiced Plantain Snack
- **Description**: Spiced fried plantain cubes with traditional spice blend
- **Price Range**: ₵12-20
- **Spice Levels**: Mild, medium, hot
- **Customizations**: Spice level, size (small, medium, large)
- **Preparation Time**: 10-15 minutes
- **Calories**: 150-180

### **Fresh Palm Wine** - Traditional Beverage
- **Description**: Naturally fermented palm wine served fresh from the palm tree
- **Price Range**: ₵15-25
- **Sizes**: Small (250ml), Medium (500ml), Large (1L)
- **Customizations**: Size, temperature (fresh cold, room temperature)
- **Preparation Time**: 5 minutes
- **Calories**: 100-120
